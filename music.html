Music has been so important in my life, i think it's something really powerful that can connect people and their emotions through this medium. I love hearing a lot of new stuff, as well as old music from the 70's, 80's...

I started listening to pop punk since I was a little kid. Bands like blink 182, Jimmy eat world, Paramore, The distillers are basically my childhood. As I grew up i did listen to more music, more genres, met new people that expanded my horizons on music. I have to thank my beautiful girlfriend because she is always helping me discover a lot of great stuff out there.

If I had to name some of my favorite artists/bands, it'd be Angels and Airwaves, Blink 182, Boy Pablo, Bring me the horizon, Porter Robinson, Mass of the fermenting Dregs, Daft Punk.
